WELCOME TO BEARY FUN GYM @ Bukit Batok!

Singapore's leading kids gym! Beary Fun Gyms has been specialising in Preschool Gymnastics Movement Programme for the past 14 years.

BearyFun Gym was first founded in 2003 and has since expanded to 4 different gyms across Singapore.

ABOUT

BearyFun Gym has coaches who are experienced in managing and coaching children of different age groups. With their knowledge of child development and needs, they are able to establish a good and warm rapport with children and their parents. BearyFun Gym has seen many children improve in their physical and cognitive abilities and received much positive feedback from parents and school.


Qualities of our BearyFun Gym coaches and classes!

- Coaching Qualification

- Teaching Qualification

- Trained in child development, child psychology, classroom management skills and first-aid skills

- Non-competitive

- Ensures that classes are structed skill based not play based

- Lesson plans allow for fundamental developmental development
