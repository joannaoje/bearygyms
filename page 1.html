Why Gym?

a gym for kids!

Beary Fun Gym holds classes for kids aged 4 to 12 years old. Classes boasts the best gymnastics class ratio and learning environment!

Benefits of gymming

Gym helps your child to grow in 3 main aspects:


1. PHYSICAL


1. Psychomotor & body coordination development

2. Increase in muscular strength, endurance, flexibility & agility

3. Knowledge of break falling techniques

4. Gain pre-requisite skills for other sports


2. COGNITIVE

1. Provides opportunities for kids to be creative

2. Stimulate their imaginations

3. Boost self-management in a safe environment

4. Increase mental focus and concentration


3. SOCIAL

1. Develop friendships in gymnastics classes

2. Gain confidence in their own abilities

3. Equip them with skills to handle physical & emotional challenges in life

4. Opportunity to communicate with peers, work within a team and engage with adults
